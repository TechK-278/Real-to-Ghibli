Features

Why Ghibli Art Generator?

A magical combination of AI and artistry that brings the Ghibli aesthetic to your photos.

🎭

Ghibli Style Transfer

Advanced diffusion models trained to capture the distinctive warmth, color palette, and painterly quality of Studio Ghibli films.

⚡

ONNX Optimized

Lightning-fast inference powered by ONNX Runtime — up to 3x faster than native PyTorch with lower memory usage.

🖼️

High Quality Output

Generate stunning 512×512 artwork that preserves the composition and details of your original photo.

🔌

Simple REST API

One endpoint. One request. Upload an image and get beautiful Ghibli art back — easy to integrate anywhere.

💻

Cross-Platform

Runs on Windows, macOS, and Linux — with CPU and GPU support. No vendor lock-in.

🛡️

Open Source
